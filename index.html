FINE ECOSYSTEM AROUND MARA RESORT

The name Mara means‘spotted place’ in masai, and we hope you agree! Mara resort is Sir Etty Jone's Kenyan Safari Camp located in the world-famous Maasai Mara ecosystem and right in the path of the annual great migration.

Mara Resort sits within the Olare Motorogi Conservancy in Nairobi which covers an area of 13,500 hectares! There are endless opportunities for game viewing, so all you need to do is sit back and let the wildlife come to you!
